Executive Summary

This market feasibility study confirms Mo Town Storage represents a sound investment opportunity in Florida's growing RV and boat storage market, with validated financial projections and competitive positioning.

Investment Verdict:

Market analysis confirms a strong 8.67% cap rate on the $8.5M investment, positioning Mo Town Storage competitively within the premium storage market segment (typical market range: 6-9%).

Market-validated rental rates across all unit categories support a gross revenue of $985,014 and an annual Net Operating Income of $736,513, confirming the financial viability of this premium storage development.

The development's premium features, climate-controlled units, and strategic location adjacent to Timber Pines provide significant competitive advantages in capturing the growing demand for high-quality boat and RV storage.
