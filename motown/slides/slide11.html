Risk Assessment & Sensitivity Analysis

Key Risk Factors

Rental Rate Achievability

Projected rates for covered storage exceed market averages. Premium units align better but are slightly above market.

Extended Lease-Up Timeline

Above-market rates could extend stabilization period, impacting early cash flows and financing covenants.

Competitive Pressure

Local competitors offer comparable amenities with established customer bases and competitive pricing.

Economic Sensitivity

RV storage sector shows resilience with demand exceeding supply despite declining new RV sales. Gap between storage demand and supply continues to widen (Toy Storage Nation, Sept 2025).

Risk Mitigation Recommendations

  • Adjust rental projections to within 10-15% of market averages
  • Phase rate increases gradually after achieving 85%+ occupancy
  • Consider flexible lease terms during initial lease-up period
  • Emphasize unique features to justify premium positioning
