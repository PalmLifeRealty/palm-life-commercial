Adjacent Market Opportunity: Timber Pines

Timber Pines, an established 55+ community with over 5,300 residents, is directly adjacent to Mo Town Storage's eastern boundary, providing a significant built-in market opportunity.

Built-In Demand Source:

Timber Pines' existing open RV/boat storage lot consistently serves its affluent retiree demographic, confirming sustained local demand for higher-quality storage solutions.

This self-described "Best Kept Secret in Florida" features a private country club lifestyle with resort-style amenities, including golf courses and clubhouses — creating an affluent customer base with high disposable income.

The community's debt-free status, manicured grounds, and low HOA fees attract financially secure retirees who often own premium RVs, boats, and collector vehicles requiring safe, convenient storage options.
