INVESTMENT OVERVIEW

Executive Summary

Motown Luxury Storage presents an investment opportunity in the rapidly growing premium RV and boat storage market in Spring Hill, Florida. This permit ready, institutional-quality facility offers a rare combination of strong market fundamentals, premium amenities, and an innovative ownership model.

Located strategically at the intersection of Trenton Avenue and US Highway 19, Motown provides unparalleled access to the recreational areas including Gulf Coast, Weeki Wachee River, numerous boat launches with prominent area car shows. The facility features climate-controlled enclosed units with private mezzanines and in-unit bathrooms, along with covered storage options for oversized vehicles—representing the highest standard of storage accommodations in the market.

With an 8.3% stabilized cap rate and strong annual NOI of $702,493 at 90% occupancy, this investment offers institutional investors an attractive risk-adjusted return in a high-demand, limited-supply asset class. The $8.5M total investment reflects core development costs; SBA financing is also available with a 1.45x DSCR, providing flexibility for both cash and leveraged buyers.
