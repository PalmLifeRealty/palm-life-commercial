Investment Thesis

Zoning Certainty Meets Location Premium

1136 Douglas Avenue represents one of the most compelling infill development opportunities in Dunedin's constrained coastal market. The site arrives with MF-15 zoning already in place, enabling up to 4 residential units by right — eliminating the entitlement risk that defines most comparable infill plays in the corridor.

Direct frontage on the Pinellas Trail — one of the most recognizable lifestyle amenities in the Tampa Bay region — positions any future product at an immediate premium over competing supply. The park adjacency to the rear creates a permanent green buffer that cannot be developed away, a characteristic that meaningfully differentiates this site from standard infill parcels.

The 4-lot assemblage includes vacated right-of-way, improving site efficiency and creating a larger effective footprint than the raw parcel count would suggest. Combined with Downtown Dunedin proximity and all utilities in place, this is a turnkey development entry in one of Florida's most supply-constrained markets.

Investment Highlights

Why This Site
Commands a Premium

Entitlement Risk Eliminated

By-right approval for up to 4 units under MF-15 zoning. No Development Order required — proceed directly to permitting. A significant advantage in a jurisdiction where entitlement timelines routinely exceed 12–18 months.

Density + Flexibility

MF-15 zoning supports townhomes, boutique multifamily, and luxury rental product. Height allowance up to ~50 ft / 5 stories enables vertical design that could capture upper-level water views not available at grade.

Pinellas Trail Frontage

Direct frontage on the Pinellas Trail creates a measurable rent and resale premium over competing inventory. Trail-facing units command lifestyle positioning that cannot be replicated on standard residential streets.

Permanent Park Buffer

Adjacent public park to the rear provides a permanent green space and privacy buffer. Unlike a neighboring lot, a park cannot be built on — making this buffer a permanent feature of the site's value proposition.

Downtown Dunedin Walkability

Located minutes from Downtown Dunedin's restaurant row, craft brewery district, marina, and waterfront parks. This is the exact walkability profile that new-construction buyers in this submarket are actively pricing in.

Utilities In Place

Water, sewer, and electric service are available, eliminating the infrastructure investment that often burdens comparable infill sites. Paved road access and site configuration support efficient construction sequencing.

Market Positioning

Coastal Market

Sites like this — MF-zoned, assembled, entitled, and in a trail-facing location — simply do not become available on a regular basis.

Dunedin's coastal boundaries and established development pattern leave virtually no meaningful infill supply. New-construction opportunities of this scale are exceptional events, not regular market occurrences.
